┌──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  ❓ TROUBLESHOOTING                                                  │
└──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  Problem: Receipt too wide
  Solution: Set printer paper size to 58mm

  Problem: Text cut off
  Solution: Reduce print scale to 95%

  Problem: Font not monospace
  Solution: Enable "Print background graphics"

  More help: THERMAL_RECEIPT_GUIDE.md → Troubleshooting section
